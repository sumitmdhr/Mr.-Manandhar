Brickwork Tips

Brick Soaking: Soak bricks in water before use to prevent moisture absorption from mortar.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these calculations?

Calculations follow Indian Standard (IS) codes and industry standards. Actual requirements may vary slightly based on site conditions and material quality.

Can I save my calculations?

Currently results aren't saved automatically. We recommend taking a screenshot or noting down the results. Future versions may include saving functionality.

Why are there different concrete grades?

Different grades (M10, M20 etc.) indicate the compressive strength of concrete in N/mm² after 28 days. Higher grades are used for structures needing more strength.
